DICE ROLLERS

There is no tutorial level yet. Push dice to a receptacle. When the die has the same number as the receptacle and it is touching the receptacle, you can push the die into it. Press 'Space Bar' to use your compass. The compass shows you what value the dice will be in the 4 adjacent positions around the current die. Press 'r' to reset the level.
